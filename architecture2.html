Express Route

Create dedicated connection from your on-premises to Azure location without going through public internet. To configure ExpressRoute follow the below steps

  • Gather information about connectivity providers and locations.
  • Create an ExpressRoute circuit.
  • Configure routing (circuit peering).
  • Link a VNet to an ExpressRoute circuit
